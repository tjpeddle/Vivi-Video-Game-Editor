🎬 Save a week as a video

The story plays right here, but to keep a shareable .mp4, do a quick screen recording while it plays:
  1. Windows: press Win + Alt + R (Xbox Game Bar) to start/stop recording.
  2. Mac: press Shift + Cmd + 5, choose “Record Selected Portion,” box the stage, hit Record.
  3. Pick the week, press ↺ Restart, flip on Narrate aloud if you want Vivi's voice too.
Tip: full-screen the browser first so the stage is big and crisp.
