🎬 Save as a video file

The film plays right here in the browser, but to keep a real .mp4 you can share, the simplest way is a quick screen recording while it plays:
  1. Windows: press Win + Alt + R (Xbox Game Bar) to start/stop recording the window.
  2. Mac: press Shift + Cmd + 5, choose “Record Selected Portion,” box the stage, and hit Record.
  3. Then come back here, open Theater, press ↺ Restart, and turn on Narrate aloud if you want Vivi's voice captured too.
Tip: full-screen your browser first so the stage is nice and big.
